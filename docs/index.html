Features

A coach, not a spreadsheet

Everything you need to train with intent — self-hosted, private, and free of metered AI bills.

Cycling load model

FTP-based zones, per-session TSS, and a Fitness / Fatigue / Form model (CTL · ATL · TSB).

Strength progression

Prescribed weight × sets × reps with RIR/RPE-driven progression and deloads.

Concurrent training

Schedules bike + strength together to minimize the interference effect.

Readiness traffic light

HRV trend, resting HR, sleep and a quick check → green / yellow / red each morning.

Conversational coach

Talk to your coach in the web app — the plan is built with you, not handed down.

FIT / GPX import

Drop activity files in the browser or a watch folder — deduplicated automatically.

Pluggable data

Garmin · Strava · Apple Health · file import — enable only the sources you have.

PWA · DE / EN

Installable on your phone, no app store. Fully bilingual interface, runs on a Raspberry Pi.

How it works

Three steps to a plan

No Docker, no cloud, no per-token bill. Runs from your terminal on hardware you own.

1

Connect your data

Run onboarding in the browser, then bring your history in — drop a FIT/GPX file, or wire up Garmin, Strava or Apple Health later. Start with nothing but a morning check if you like.

2

Talk to your coach

Tell Sirdar your goal and constraints. It asks questions, weighs your load and readiness, and writes a periodized plan — explaining the why behind every session.

3

Train the prescribed plan

Open "Today" for exact intervals, watts and weights. Each morning the readiness light re-tunes the session, and a missed day rebalances the week instead of piling up.

The training brain

Sport science, enforced

The AI works inside hard guardrails — so it builds you up without digging a hole.

🚴

FTP zones & TSS

Every ride is anchored to your FTP — power zones and a training-stress score per session, so load is comparable day to day.

📈

CTL / ATL / TSB + ramp-rate guardrail

A rolling fitness / fatigue / form model with a hard cap of ≤ +3–5 CTL/week so progression stays sustainable.

🔀

Concurrent-training rules

Leg-heavy strength is spaced from key bike sessions. Bundle the hard days, bundle the easy days, sequence by your primary goal.

🚦

Readiness auto-regulation

🟢 train as planned · 🟡 soften the hard one · 🔴 swap for recovery and rebalance.

Zero API cost, by design — 0 €

Sirdar never calls a metered API. It drives the Claude Code CLI you already pay for, so your Claude Max/Pro subscription is the only "AI bill" — the same login you use in your terminal.
